Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

+

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer service line directly for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a video call instead of visiting your office?

+

Absolutely! We offer virtual consultations via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Google Meet. Simply mention your preference for a video call when submitting your inquiry, and we'll arrange it at a time that works for you.

Do I need to make an appointment to visit your office?

+

While walk-ins are welcome during regular business hours, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the appropriate specialist is available to assist you. This helps us prepare relevant information in advance and provide you with the best service.

How can I get updates on new property listings?

+

You can sign up for our property alerts by creating an account on our website. This allows you to set specific criteria for properties you're interested in, and you'll receive automatic notifications when matching properties become available.

Do you offer services in other languages?

+

Yes, our team includes agents who speak multiple languages including Spanish, French, Mandarin, and Arabic. Please let us know your language preference when contacting us, and we'll connect you with an appropriate agent.
