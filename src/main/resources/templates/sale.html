Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about selling your property with HomyZ

What is the best time to sell my property?

+

While spring and summer are traditionally considered the best seasons to sell properties, the ideal time to sell depends on various factors including local market conditions, property type, and your personal circumstances. Our agents can provide a customized analysis of the best time to list your specific property.

How do you determine the listing price for my property?

+

We determine the optimal listing price through a comprehensive market analysis that takes into account recent comparable sales in your area, current market conditions, the unique features and condition of your property, and buyer demand. Our goal is to set a competitive price that will attract buyers while maximizing your return.

What improvements should I make before selling?

+

Not all improvements offer equal returns when selling. We recommend focusing on cost-effective improvements that can enhance your property's appeal without excessive spending. These typically include basic repairs, fresh paint, deep cleaning, decluttering, and enhancing curb appeal. Our agents will provide personalized recommendations based on your specific property.

How long will it take to sell my property?

+

The selling timeline varies based on market conditions, property location, price point, and property condition. In a balanced market, properly priced homes typically sell within 30-60 days. However, luxury properties or homes in less active markets may take longer. We'll provide you with a realistic timeline based on current market trends and your specific property.

What are the costs involved in selling a property?

+

The main costs when selling include real estate agent commissions (typically 5-6% of the sale price), closing costs (1-3%), potential repairs or improvements, staging costs, and possibly overlap costs if you're buying a new home. We provide a detailed cost breakdown upfront so you can plan accordingly and understand your potential net proceeds.

Do I need to be present for property showings?

+

No, you do not need to be present for showings. In fact, it's generally recommended that sellers are not present during showings, as this allows potential buyers to feel more comfortable exploring and discussing the property. Our agents will coordinate and conduct all showings on your behalf, providing you with feedback afterward.
